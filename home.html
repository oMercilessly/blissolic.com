What is TicketMaster?



TicketMaster is the central bot for all your ticketing needs. Create panels at the click of a button. Let your users do what ever they need, simply ready for them at the push of a button. With an amazing support team, working devs and constructive feedback, we can all push TicketMaster to the limits.

Features

Easy Setup

TicketMaster provides with incredibly easy setup. Get your ticketing panels ready at the click of a button. TicketMaster also introduces incredibly simple-to-use configuration settings, with flexibility being at the core of the bot.

Amazing features

With 15+ commands at the moment, more and more are coming soon. After all, we need you to help us improve. Check out the official Support Server for changelogs, new updates, exclusive events and even giveaways!

Consistent Updates

Trust us; more and more updates will be coming your way ASAP. We rely on your feedback to thrive and to make TicketMaster better than what it is. If you would like to help us improve the bot, please send some suggestions in our discord!
